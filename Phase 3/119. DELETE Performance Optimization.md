## 6. Common Patterns

Common patterns in 119. DELETE Performance Optimization include:
* **Batch Deletion**: Deleting large amounts of data in batches to reduce the overhead of individual delete operations.
* **Index Rebuilding**: Rebuilding indexes after deletion to maintain optimal index performance.
* **Transaction Log Rotation**: Rotating transaction logs to prevent log growth and maintain system performance.

## 7. Anti-Patterns

Anti-patterns in 119. DELETE Performance Optimization include:
* **Individual Deletion**: Deleting data one record at a time, leading to performance degradation.
* **Inadequate Indexing**: Failing to maintain optimal indexing, resulting in slow delete performance.
* **Insufficient Transaction Log Management**: Failing to manage transaction logs, leading to log growth and system performance issues.
