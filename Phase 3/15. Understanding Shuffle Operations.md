## 3. Terminology and Definitions
| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Shuffle | A operation that rearranges data elements to achieve a specific goal, such as improving data locality or reducing communication overhead. |
| Partitioning | The process of dividing data into smaller, more manageable chunks, often to facilitate parallel processing or storage. |
| Permutation | A specific type of shuffle operation that rearranges data elements in a particular order, often to achieve a desired property, such as sorted order. |
