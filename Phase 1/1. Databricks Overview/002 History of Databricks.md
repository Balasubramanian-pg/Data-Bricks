## Core Concepts

The History of Databricks topic is built around the following core concepts:

### Founding and Early Days
Databricks was founded in 2013 by the original creators of Apache Spark, including Ali Ghodsi, Andy Konwinski, Ion Stoica, and Matei Zaharia. The company's early focus was on developing a cloud-based platform that would simplify the use of Apache Spark for data processing and analytics.

### Key Product Releases and Feature Developments
Databricks has released several key products and features over the years, including Databricks Cloud, Databricks Delta, and Databricks Lakehouse. These releases have significantly expanded the capabilities of the platform and improved its usability.
